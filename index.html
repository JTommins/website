About Me

I'm a writer, GIS enthusiast, and natural resources professional with a background in policy, journalism, ditch digging, and chainsaws. My work explores the intersections of conservation, land, and outdoor culture, with a focus on the lived experiences of those who protect our natural heritage. I hope to inform, engage, and advocate for deeper connections between people and the landscapes they rely on.
